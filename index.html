About me

Educational background and work experience

My name is Grazyna Radzka, I come from Poland and for 15 years now my greatest passion is teaching foreign languages. I’m an educated German and Russian teacher and I’m also fluent in English. I have plenty of experience in teaching foreign languages both in Poland and abroad.

Current focus

Currently I teach Polish and German, globally via Skype and locally in the Polish city Gorzow Wielkopolski. My students are people of all age groups and at all language levels.

My speciality is teaching adults who use the foreign language at work or prepare to work abroad.

Effectiveness

If you wish to learn Polish or German fast and effective you are in the right place!

Results will come just after the first lessons because starting from our first meeting we will use only foreign language and the handouts for each lesson will be created based on your individual language goals and interests.
